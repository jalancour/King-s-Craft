About King's Craft

Jesse Lancour · Owner

King's Craft Handyman Services is a professional handyman company based in Fort Pierce, Florida, owned and operated by Jesse Lancour. We serve renters, homeowners and businesses throughout St. Lucie County and the surrounding areas including Martin County, Indian River County, and northern Palm Beach County.

Jesse built King's Craft on three core principles:

Honest Partnership
Diligent Service
Excellent Craft

He spent 9 years in Haiti with his family serving the population with these same qualities. Every job — whether a ceiling fan swap or a custom built-in — is approached with the same attention to detail and respect for your home. We show up on time, communicate clearly, clean up before we leave, and charge a fair price.

King's Craft is fully covered by Commercial General Liability insurance. We handle a wide range of handyman services including repairs and replacements, interior and exterior installations, furniture and cabinet assembly, LVP flooring, drywall, fixture replacements, lawn maintenance, pressure washing, and outdoor additions. No job is too small.

The Lancour Family · Nov 2025

Jesse's vision for King's Craft goes beyond a decent salary for his family. The larger vision for the company is to become a handyman school. Young men will begin by apprenticing alongside Jesse — not only learning how to do the physical work with excellent craft but understanding the importance of strong character. We aim to build a community of handymen that honor their customers and that people can trust in their homes.

If you're curious about the work in Haiti the Lancours were and still are involved in, head over to cornerstonehaiti.org to check it out!
