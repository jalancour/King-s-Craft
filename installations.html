What We Do

Installations & Assembly

Whether you just moved in or finally got around to that project, King's Craft gets things installed correctly the first time. We serve renters, homeowners, and businesses throughout St. Lucie County and surrounding areas. Show up on time, work clean, leave it right.

TVs, Pictures & Mirrors

Wall mounting for flat-screen TVs of all sizes, artwork, and mirrors. We locate studs, use the right hardware, and make sure everything is level and secure.

Blinds, Shades & Drapery

Installation of window blinds, roller shades, cellular shades, and drapery hardware. Precise measurements and clean mounting for a finished look.

Wall AC Units & Appliances

Installation of wall and window air conditioning units, as well as kitchen and laundry appliances. Proper securing and connection for safe, efficient operation.

Furniture, Cabinets & Beds

Assembly of flat-pack and boxed furniture, cabinets, bed frames, and shelving units. We follow manufacturer instructions carefully and make sure everything is solid.

Closet Organization

Installation of closet systems, shelving, rods, and organizers. We turn cluttered closets into functional, well-organized storage spaces.

Above Ground Pools

Assembly and setup of above ground swimming pools including frame, liner, and pump connections. Get your pool ready for the Florida summer without the headache.
